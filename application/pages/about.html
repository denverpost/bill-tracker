About the Colorado Bill Tracker

Colorado Bill Tracker publishes the legislative happenings of the Colorado state senate and state house. Each Tuesday through Saturday when legislature is in session the site updates with what happened to which legislation from the previous day. The site is updated with data provided by the Sunlight Foundation. Questions? Comments? Technical issues? Email Joe Murphy.
